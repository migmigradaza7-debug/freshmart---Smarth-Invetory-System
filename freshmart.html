Project Purpose

The purpose of this initiative is to develop a digital inventory tracking software program for FreshMart which will enable the company to more easily monitor their inventory levels, sales patterns, and delivery schedules from suppliers on an ongoing basis. The digital inventory system will allow us to significantly lower our waste levels, reduce our overstocking of inventory, improve efficiencies in our operations via alerts and analytics, and provide managers with an easy to use dashboard where they can view reports and manage orders.
